## Editorial rules

- `FACT`: directly stated in one or both reference PDFs.
- `REWRITE`: wording tightened, reordered, or grouped without changing the claim.
- `INFERENCE`: a presentation decision derived from the evidence, not a new fact.
- `UNCERTAIN`: retained as source-stated or omitted when the PDFs do not make it safe to resolve.
- All dates use the source's month/year precision. `Present` is preserved as written in the references; no current-month update is inferred.
- The source uses both `visualisation` and the typo `Scrapping`; canonical copy keeps British spelling and corrects the obvious spelling to `scraping`.
