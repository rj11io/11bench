## Evidence labels

- **[F] Fact:** directly present in one or both PDFs.
- **[R] Rewrite:** clearer or more concise language that does not add a claim.
- **[I] Inference:** a cautious editorial conclusion supported by multiple facts.
- **[U] Uncertainty:** unresolved or insufficiently supported; not silently promoted to fact.

## Reconciliation principles

1. `RJ_CV_max.pdf` supplies the fullest chronology and detailed project descriptions.
2. `RJ_CV.pdf` supplies the candidate's own two-page prioritization and often the safest concise wording.
3. When the documents differ only in detail, the longer version is treated as elaboration.
4. Exact names, employers, links, dates, branded product names, and credentials are preserved.
5. Rewrites improve action, clarity, and scanability but do not introduce metrics, scale, revenue, team size, user counts, or causal outcomes absent from the PDFs.
