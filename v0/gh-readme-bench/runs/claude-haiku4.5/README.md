## About

I started coding young—modding games, reverse-engineering consoles, building a fighting game on MUGEN, and running Counter-Strike servers. At 14, I led a robotics team to second place nationally and the finals of the 2008 world cup in China using LEGO Mindstorms. Competitive gaming taught me what leadership and remote team coordination really look like.

I went professional in 2015 and specialized in TypeScript frontend applications. For the past decade, I've built **dashboards, data platforms, and proprietary explorers** for cybersecurity, crypto, and gaming companies—where I found a deep passion for data-driven products and polished visualizations.

On most projects, I was the **first frontend hire**: owning architecture, tooling, component libraries, and pipelines from day one, then growing teams around them.

Today I'm focused on **AI product engineering**. I've built with AI since the first releases of Copilot and ChatGPT, moving from prompt engineering to designing full agent harnesses. I work as a hands-on freelancer across multiple teams, shipping AI extraction tools, custom GPT experiences, threat-intelligence dashboards, and open-source AI skills.

I operate as a self-guided missile: point me at a target and I'll figure out how to hit it.
