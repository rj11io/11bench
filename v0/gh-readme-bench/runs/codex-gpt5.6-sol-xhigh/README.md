## The pattern behind the projects

- **Build the foundation.** On most projects I was the first frontend hire,
  owning architecture, tooling, component libraries, infrastructure, and
  pipelines from day one.
- **Make the system legible.** Much of my career has been spent on dashboards,
  product platforms, and proprietary data explorers across cybersecurity,
  crypto, and gaming.
- **Leave the team stronger.** I hire, interview, onboard, document, and write
  the playbooks that help new engineers integrate without guesswork.

## Before the job titles

I started by modding and reverse-engineering games and consoles, built a
fighting game with MUGEN, and ran dedicated Counter-Strike and Minecraft
servers. At 14, programming LEGO Mindstorms robots helped my team place second
nationally and reach the final four of the 2008 robotics world cup in China.

The tools changed. The instinct did not: understand the system, build the
interface, and help the team operate it.
