- Education: credible but lower priority after a decade-long professional track; available in the CV.
- Exact employer locations and “Remote/B2B” labels on every role: repetitive; Lisbon and current B2B work are retained.
- Weekly “TED” talks, detailed OMEGA feature names, in-house Phantasma utilities, Coalition marketing pages, and Glaiveware business disciplines: useful interview detail, not first-scan profile material.
- GitHub follower/star/repository counts and contribution streaks: not supplied by the PDFs, volatile, and not part of the intended value proposition.
