## Editorial choices

### Audience and hierarchy

Primary readers are potential open-source collaborators, early-stage teams, and technically curious GitHub visitors. Their likely first questions are: Who is this? What are they building now? Where is the work? How did they earn this focus? The README answers those in that order.

### Voice and opening hook

Voice: precise, quietly confident, technically literate, human, and non-CV-like. Avoid superlatives and generic “passionate about” language.

Opening hook: **“I build the part where AI systems, product interfaces, and data meet—and make it usable.”** This is editorial synthesis of stated experience, not a claim of a particular product, metric, or methodology.

### Calls to action

- Primary: explore the work at `rj11.io`.

- Contact: the public email printed in both CVs, `ricardojorgexyz@gmail.com`.
